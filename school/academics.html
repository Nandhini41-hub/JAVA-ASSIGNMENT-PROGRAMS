Academic Programs

JAI MATRICULATION HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL offers a diverse range of academic programs designed to meet the needs of every student.

Our curriculum includes advanced placement courses, arts, sciences, and technology programs.

Our Advanced Placement (AP) program provides students with the opportunity to take college-level courses and exams while still in high school.

  • AP Mathematics
  • AP Science
  • AP English Literature
  • AP History
  • AP Computer Science

Arts Program

Our Arts Program offers a creative outlet for students interested in visual arts, music, and drama.

  • Visual Arts: Painting, Sculpture, Photography
  • Music: Band, Choir, Orchestra
  • Drama: Theater Productions, Acting Workshops
