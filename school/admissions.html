Admissions Information

We welcome students from all backgrounds to apply to JAI MATRICULATION HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL. Our admissions process is designed to be inclusive and accessible.

For more details on how to apply, please contact our admissions office or visit the campus.

Admission Requirements

To apply for admission, please ensure you meet the following requirements:

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of the most recent report card
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Birth certificate or passport copy

Schedule a Campus Tour

We encourage prospective students and their families to visit our campus and experience the vibrant community at JAI MATRICULATION HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL. Campus tours are available by appointment.
